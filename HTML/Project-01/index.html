Taco Trivia

when did taco first appear in the US?

The Taco was first introduced to the United States in 1905.(Source: Smithsonian Magazine) Mexican migrants were coming in to work on railroads and other jobs and started to bring their delicious food with them. Tacos were essentially a street food at this time since they were highly portable and cheap.
